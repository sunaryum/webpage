Proof of Energy

1. Connect Your MicroNode

Install the Sunaryum MicroNode on your solar panel. Enter your wallet address to bind your energy production to your identity. Each kWh measurement is signed using your private key, ensuring authenticity and ownership.

2. Secure Upload at Day’s End

At midnight, the MicroNode compiles hourly readings into a Merkle Tree and transmits the data to a Full Node. Each submission is timestamped, signed, and queued for validation.

[Flowchart: Merkle Tree → Full Node]

3. Proof-of-Energy Consensus

Each night, a Full Node is selected through Verifiable Random Function (VRF) to propose the next block. This Proof-of-Energy consensus validates solar production with cryptographic fairness—no mining, just meaningful output.

[Widget: VRF-Based Node Selection]

4. Instant $SUN Minting

Once confirmed, each kWh is transformed into $SUN tokens and sent directly to your wallet. No fees, no intermediaries. You earn purely for the energy you’ve generated.

[Visual: Energy → Token → Wallet]

5. Use Anywhere in the Network

Your $SUN tokens can be used across Sunaryum’s smart city infrastructure—power EV stations, sell energy credits, or stake to support decentralized infrastructure. Your energy becomes usable currency.
